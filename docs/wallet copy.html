Managing Ordinals is currently very hard and any mistake can cause you to lose all your NFTs. I recommend to follow the instructions below and create a new wallet, only used for receiving Bitcoin Cows. Do your own research and proceed at your own risk.

These instructions will show you how to install Hiro Wallet and create an ordinal receiver address needed to reserve Bitcoin Cows. You can find alternative/mobile wallets further below.

  1. Install Hiro Wallet in your browser as an extension from either Chrome or Firefox.
  2. Once the wallet is launched generate a new wallet for self-custody of the inscriptions you‚Äôll receive, making sure to back up your Secret Key (i.e. seed phrase) safely.
  3. Select ‚ÄúAdd new‚Äù under ‚ÄúCollectibles‚Äù on the home screen to copy a fresh deposit address for your new inscription. Do not use the normal receive Bitcoin address.

Wallets ‚Äã

  • Hiro

    • More difficult to use

    • Recommended wallet
    • Very hard to use and requires 600-700gb+ of storage
    • Takes multiple days to install

Transferring NFTs ‚Äã

I would not yet trust any of these wallets with correctly transferring NFTs, especially if there are multiple in one wallet. Wait for other people to test and share their experiences. We are still very early. A reservation will send all tokens to one wallet and even if your wallet says they support transfers, it might only support transferring if there is only one NFT in the wallet.
This means that you may have to wait until wallet capabilities are upgraded before you can transfer your Bitcoin Cows. Therefore, do not expect a "quick flip" without the risks described above.
